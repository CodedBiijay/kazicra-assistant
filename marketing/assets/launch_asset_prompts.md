# Launch Asset Prompts (ExoCore Brand)

The following prompts are designed for the `nano-banana-imager` (Gemini 3 Pro) to generate high-fidelity launch assets for MonitorFlow.

## Brand Palette
- **Background**: Ebony `#1A1A1B`
- **Text**: Bone `#F9F6EE`
- **Accent**: Picasso Green `#D0F0C0`
- **Alert**: Coral `#FF4D4D`

---

### Asset 1: Carousel Cover (The Hook)
**File**: `launch_carousel_cover.png`
**Prompt**:
> A high-quality social media carousel cover for LinkedIn. Minimalist, premium dark mode aesthetic. Background is deep matte ebony (#1A1A1B). Text is large, bold, sans-serif in bone white (#F9F6EE) reading "The Annual Review Scramble". There is a subtle, glowing 'Picasso Green' (#D0F0C0) underline or accent element. A translucent glassmorphism card floats in the background. The style is 'ExoCore Systems' brand: professional, clinical, tech-forward, elite. No clutter.

---

### Asset 2: Infographic (The Bridge/Growth)
**File**: `launch_infographic_growth.png`
**Prompt**:
> An infographic design titled "The CRA Evolution". Visual style: Dark mode, sleek data visualization. Background is deep ebony (#1A1A1B). A glowing staircase or arrow pointing upwards, transitioning from "Task Execution" (bottom, faint grey) to "Strategic Oversight" (top, bright Picasso Green #D0F0C0). Icons representing "Site Focus" vs "Multi-Site Strategy". The aesthetic is schematic, blueprint-like, high-tech. Text is crisp and legible in bone white (#F9F6EE).

---

### Asset 3: Typography Poster (The Future)
**File**: `launch_poster_future.png`
**Prompt**:
> A bold typographic poster design. Large, massive text "MONITOR FLOW" taking up most of the space, cropped or overflowing, in a "Space Grotesk" style font. Color is bone white (#F9F6EE) on an ebony background (#1A1A1B). A small "ExoCore Systems" badge in Picasso Green (#D0F0C0) in the corner. Grainy texture overlay (noise). Innovative, brutalist but refined web design aesthetic.
